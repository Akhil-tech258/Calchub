Our story

About CalcHub

CalcHub started with a simple idea: the internet has hundreds of ad-heavy, slow, cluttered calculator sites — and not enough fast, clean, trustworthy ones. So we built one place with over 100 calculators covering finance, health, school, math, engineering, programming and everyday life.

What makes CalcHub different

  • No sign-up, no paywalls, no intrusive ads
  • Every calculation runs locally in your browser — nothing is sent to a server
  • Installable as an app and usable offline
  • Built with plain HTML, CSS and JavaScript for speed and reliability

Our commitment to accuracy

Every calculator uses standard, well-documented formulas. That said, tools here are meant for planning and estimation. For decisions with real financial, medical or legal weight, please consult a qualified professional.
