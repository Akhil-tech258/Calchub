Frequently asked questions

Is CalcHub really free to use?

Yes. Every calculator on CalcHub is completely free, with no sign-up, no paywalls, and no limits on how often you use them.

Do I need an internet connection?

Once you've visited CalcHub, it installs as a Progressive Web App and most calculators keep working offline — great for spotty connections.

Is my data stored anywhere?

No. All calculations run locally in your browser. Favorites and recent history are saved only on your device using local storage.

How accurate are the results?

Every calculator uses standard, well-established formulas. Financial and health calculators are meant for planning and estimation — always confirm important decisions with a qualified professional.

Can I suggest a new calculator?

Absolutely — use the contact page to send a request and we'll consider it for a future update.
