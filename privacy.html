What we collect

CalcHub performs all calculations locally in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store the numbers you enter into any calculator.

Local storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember your theme preference, favorite calculators, and recently used calculators. This information stays on your device and is never sent to a server.

Analytics

If analytics are enabled on this deployment, they collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as page views) to help us understand which calculators are useful. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Pages on CalcHub may link to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of CalcHub after changes means you accept the updated policy.
